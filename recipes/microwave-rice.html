Microwave Rice

The bomb ass rice

We all have them, those lazy nights where we don't feel like cooking.With 'recipe' we will making microwave rice, because we are broke

Ingredients

  • Microwave rice
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Microwave rice for 3 minutes or as per back of package
  2. Salt and Pepper to taste
  3. Enjoy :) !!!!
